## üí° **KEY BUSINESS INSIGHTS**

### **Churn Drivers:**
- Library closes/reduces operations
- Competitor with better pricing
- Lack of needed features
- Poor support experience

### **Growth Drivers:**
- Successful customer referrals
- Feature expansion (AI, integrations)
- Market expansion (new cities)
- WhatsApp Business adoption

### **Profit Margins:**
- **Subscription:** High margin (80-90%)
- **Credits:** Medium margin (40-60%) - SMS/WhatsApp gateway costs
- **Support:** Cost center, but critical for retention
